About Our Company

We Execute Our Ideas From The Start To Finish

MyHRSpace (Pty) Ltd is a 100% citizen-owned company with the sole aim of assisting people to reach their potential by using scientifically proven behavioral interventions. We provide a one-stop service that includes diagnosis, consulting, project management and implementation. All our services are provided Ina manner that supports simplicity, cost effectiveness, and the necessary knowledge transfer that allows the client to be self-sufficient. We have extensive experience In all facets of behavioral science, human resource management, executive coaching, Learning & development, organizational development and industrial/organizational psychology.

Mooketsi Founder, Ceo

Profile Of Managing Consultant

Mooketsi Maphane is a registered Industrial Psychologist with the Botswana Professional Health Council. He brings over 15 years of executive-level expertise In strategic human resource management, learning & development, talent management & acquisition, process design, change management, strategic planning, organizational alignment, mergers & acquisitions, executive & Board compensation, succession planning, external/internal communications.
